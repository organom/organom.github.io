After setting up a [Multiboard tool wall](/2026-02-14-from-gridfinity-drawers-to-a-multiboard-tool-wall/) in the server room, I wanted to extend the system to other spots around the office. The IKEA Kallax shelves were an obvious target — plenty of flat side panels going to waste.

The key was finding a mounting solution that doesn't damage the furniture. The [Kallax connector for Multiboard](https://makerworld.com/en/models/730813-kallax-connector-for-multiboard) clips onto the Kallax frame without screws or adhesive, making it fully reversible.

Near the gaming corner, a Kallax became the perfect place to hold the gaming controllers, tv remote and headsets, keeping keep everything visible and grab-ready.



Another Kallax near the coffee machine and whiteboard got the same treatment. This one holds whiteboard markers, business cards, a Steam controller, and a few 3D printed figures — small items that would otherwise clutter the desk.

{% include image-gallery.html folder="/assets/posts/2025-12-26-multiboard-kallax" %}

Both setups are fully reversible. No screws in the furniture, no damage, and easy to reconfigure whenever the collection grows.
