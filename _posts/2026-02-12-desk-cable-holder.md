Simple problem, simple fix. Charging cables kept sliding off the desk whenever I unplugged a device. The [desk cable holder with clamp](https://makerworld.com/en/models/2001262-desk-cable-holder-cable-organizer-with-clamp) clips directly onto the desk edge — no adhesive, no screws, fully removable.

It holds five cables at once, each in its own slot. I've got micro USB, mini USB, USB-C, and a barrel connector all parked and ready to grab. The clamp grip is solid enough that the holder stays put even when pulling cables out.
